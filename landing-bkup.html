Mars Art

Exploring the beauty of the red planet from it's surface

Surface of Mars

Each Rover that NASA has landed on Mars has been equipped with cameras. Over the generations of rovers, those cameras captured stunning images ranging from stark black and white pictures of an alien landscape to images of great scientific importance allowing us to peer into the history of the red planet. The Mars Art project aims to explore the beauty of the red planet by making rover images easier to search through and view.
