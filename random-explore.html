Exploring the Surface of Mars

NASA has landed several rovers on Mars.

  • Sojorner
  • Spirit
  • Opportunity
  • Curiosity
  • Perseverance

Each of these rovers were equipped cameras that served several purposes, from navigation to scientific exploration. Over the generations of rovers, those cameras captured stunning images ranging from stark black and white pictures of an alien landscape to images of great scientific importance allowing us to peer into the history of the red planet.
